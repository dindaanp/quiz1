Tulungagung

Tulungagung is surrounded by mountains and stunning beaches. One of its main attractions is Sine Beach, with its fine white sand and clear sea water, making it perfect for relaxing or playing in the water. The presence of Mount Wilis also offers challenging hiking tracks, taking you to spectacular views from a height. Rich Culture and Traditions Tulungagung's culture is enriched with various unique traditions, one of which is the "Rebo Wekasan" ceremony which is celebrated every year. In this celebration, people gather to pray and give thanks, while enjoying traditional entertainment such as dance and gamelan music. Local arts such as keris and batik are also an inseparable part of Tulungagung's cultural heritage. Appetizing Cuisine It would be incomplete if we talked about Tulungagung without mentioning its cuisine. This district is famous for its traditional dishes, such as crispy Tahu Tempe Tulungagung and Soto Tulungagung which is rich in flavor. Don't forget to try Pecel which is served with a special peanut sauce, making every mouthful full of flavor. Local Wisdom and Friendliness of its People The people of Tulungagung are known for their friendliness. In every corner of the city, you will be greeted with warm smiles and friendly greetings. Their local wisdom is reflected in their simple way of life and mutual respect, making your travel experience more memorable.
